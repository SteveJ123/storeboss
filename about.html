About Us

StoreBoss is a native app designed and developed by a store manager who has run various stores for various companies for nearly 20 years successfully. His experience in the retail environment and self-taught technology skills has culminated in the birth of StoreBoss. Having dealt with the frustrations of a growing responsibilty as retail management evolved over 2 decades, this app became necessary to both work for the millennial manager as well as the old school manager, for whom, keeping up with the extra workload has just become too much. The app is a tool to manage all your departments and their teams in one place.
